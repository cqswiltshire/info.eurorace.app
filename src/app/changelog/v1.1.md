# Release Notes — V1.1 (2026-07-14)

### ✨ What's New

- **Smarter Location Search**: Finding specific hotels, train stations, and obscure landmarks is now much more reliable when logging journeys or setting checkpoints — particularly for places that don't appear in standard postcode searches.

- **Fairer Expense Scoring on Multi-Leg Trips**: If your race uses different teams on different legs, the expense scoring curve now correctly accounts for this. Teams that weren't active on a given leg are no longer included in the comparison, keeping the competition fair for those who actually participated.

- **Improved Photo Gallery Instructions**: AI-generated scoring rules and instructions now display cleanly in the Photo Gallery, with properly formatted lists and line breaks that are much easier to read.

- **Journey Scheduling Improvements**: Timetables and station matching now correctly handle local timezones across different countries, ensuring your scheduled journeys stay accurate wherever you are in Europe.

### 🐛 Fixes

- **Itinerary View for Followers**: Fixed an issue where users following a trip could be unable to see the full itinerary route even after logging in.

- **Score Integrity & Stability**: Fixed an internal error during the integrity audit that prevented the tool from properly reporting underlying data issues, added safety checks to prevent background score calculations from silently failing on malformed trip data, resolved a rare crash when opening the checkpoint editor for submissions with incomplete location data, and fixed minor internal typing and build-related issues.
