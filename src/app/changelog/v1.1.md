# Release Notes — V1.1 (2026-07-15)

### ✨ What's New

- **Smarter Location Search**: Finding specific hotels, train stations, and obscure landmarks is now much more reliable when logging journeys or setting checkpoints — particularly for places that don't appear in standard postcode searches.

- **Expense Scoring for Trips with Different Teams on Each Leg**: If your race uses different teams on different legs, the expense scoring now excludes teams that weren't active on a given leg, keeping the competition fair for those who actually participated.

- **Improved Photo Gallery Instructions**: AI-generated scoring rules and instructions now display cleanly in the Photo Gallery, with properly formatted lists and line breaks that are much easier to read.

- **Journey Scheduling Improvements**: Timetables and station matching now correctly handle local timezones across different countries, ensuring your journey inforation stays accurate wherever you are in Europe.

- **Legal Navigation**: Added links to the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service on the System Info page and during the licence purchase process where the user must accept them before purchasing a licence.

- **Website**: Added a link to the Euro Race website on the System Info page.

### 🐛 Fixes

- **Live Status for Completed Races**: Fixed an issue where fully completed races using different teams for each leg would incorrectly show the finishing photos from the *first* leg on the Live Status board instead of the final destination.

- **Itinerary View for Followers**: Fixed an issue where guest users following a trip were unable to see the full itinerary.

- **Score Integrity & Stability**: Added checks to prevent background score calculations from silently failing on malformed trip data.

- **Stability**: Resolved a rare condition when opening the checkpoint editor for submissions with incomplete location data.
